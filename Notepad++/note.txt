To convert LF (Unix) line endings to CRLF (Windows) in Notepad++, follow these steps:

Method 1: Using the Menu (Recommended)
Open your file in Notepad++
Show line endings (optional but helpful):
Click View → Show Symbol → Show End of Line
You'll see CRLF, LF, or CR indicators
Convert all line endings at once:
Click Edit → EOL Conversion
Select Windows (CR LF)

Method 2: Status Bar Quick Conversion
Look at the bottom status bar of Notepad++
Click on the current line ending indicator (e.g., "Unix (LF)")
Select Windows (CR LF) from the popup menu
